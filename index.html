About me

I am currently a PhD candidate in the Department of Computer Science at University of Manchester, advised by Prof. Xiao-Jun Zeng, and I'm a member of Machine Learning and Robotics (MLR) group. Prior to that, I received my bachelor's degree from Yuan Longping Class of Hanhong Honors College at Southwest University, advised by Prof. Yong Deng and Prof. Fuyuan Xiao. My PhD research focuses on machine learning, graph representation learning and time series forecasting, which is funded by the prestigious President's Doctoral Scholor (PDS) Award.
